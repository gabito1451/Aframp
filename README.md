# Aframp

**Don't Trust, Verify**

## Overview

Aframp is a payment platform designed specifically for the African market, providing seamless cryptocurrency onramp and offramp services using African stablecoins. Built on Stellar and compatible with other major blockchains, Aframp bridges the gap between traditional African payment systems and the decentralized finance ecosystem.

## What is Aframp?

Aframp enables users across Africa to easily convert between local fiat currencies and cryptocurrencies, with a focus on African stablecoins for price stability and reliability. Whether you're looking to enter the crypto space, cash out your digital assets, or pay bills using cryptocurrency, Aframp provides a trusted, transparent platform built on the principle: **Don't Trust, Verify**.

## Key Features

### Onramp & Offramp Services
Convert between your local African currency and cryptocurrencies with ease. Support for multiple payment methods including mobile money, bank transfers, and local payment providers.

### African Stablecoin Integration
Leverage African stablecoins pegged to local currencies for stable, predictable transactions without the volatility of traditional cryptocurrencies.

### Multi-Blockchain Support
- Primary integration with Stellar blockchain for fast, low-cost transactions
- Cross-chain compatibility with Ethereum, Bitcoin, and other major networks
- Seamless asset bridging between supported blockchains

### Bill Payment Services
Pay your utilities, airtime, internet, TV subscriptions, and other bills directly using cryptocurrency. No need to convert back to fiat first.

### Transparent & Verifiable
Every transaction is recorded on-chain and can be independently verified. We operate on transparency, allowing users to validate all operations themselves.

## How It Works

### For Onramp (Fiat to Crypto)
1. Create an account and complete verification
2. Choose your local currency and desired cryptocurrency
3. Select your payment method (mobile money, bank transfer, etc.)
4. Receive cryptocurrency directly to your wallet
5. Verify the transaction on the blockchain

### For Offramp (Crypto to Fiat)
1. Initiate a withdrawal request
2. Send cryptocurrency to the provided address
3. Receive local currency via your preferred method
4. Verify the transaction completion

### For Bill Payments
1. Select the bill you want to pay (utilities, airtime, subscriptions)
2. Enter payment details and amount
3. Pay using your preferred cryptocurrency
4. Receive confirmation and receipt
